Final Project Idea: Electronic Music Box

One idea I have is to make an electronic music box, similar to the one in the video below. The music box in the video makes use of a microcontroller that controls a small MP3 module player. If I were to make this, I would want the music to start playing when I open the box and stop when I close the box using a detector switch. I like the feature of the box in the video where it plays the next song when the box is closed and reopened. I think it would also be fun to lasercut some interesting designs onto the box. This might make the project too complex, but I was also interested in the idea of adding LED lights that would react to the music like in the second video below.
